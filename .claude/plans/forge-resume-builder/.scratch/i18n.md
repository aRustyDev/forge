# Internationalization

## Resume / CV differences

- **United States/Canada**: Focus on a concise 1–2 page resume, strictly excluding personal details like age, marital status, or photos to prevent discrimination.
- **Europe** (e.g., Germany, France): Often use a detailed "CV" or the Europass CV format, which often requires a photo, date of birth, and nationality.
- **Asia/Middle East**: Resumes can be more detailed, often requiring personal information like marital status and, in some cases, religion or specific identification numbers.
- **UK/Australia**: Use "CVs" that are similar to U.S. resumes in brevity, but they typically omit personal data similar to the U.S
