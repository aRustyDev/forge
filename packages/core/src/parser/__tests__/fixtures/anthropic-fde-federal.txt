Forward Deployed Engineer, Applied AI (Federal Civilian)

About The Role

As a member of the Applied AI team at Anthropic, you will be a Forward Deployed Engineer (FDE) who embeds directly with strategic federal civilian (and occasionally state/local) customers to drive transformational AI adoption. You will collaborate closely with customer teams to ship advanced AI applications that solve real world business problems.

Core Responsibilities

- Work within customer systems to build production applications with Claude models
- Deliver technical artifacts like MCP servers, sub-agents, and agent skills for production workflows
- Provide white glove deployment support for Anthropic products in federal government environments
- Identify and codify repeatable deployment patterns and contribute insights back to Product and Engineering
- Maintain strong knowledge of latest LLM capabilities, implementation patterns, and AI product development stacks
- Build long term relationships with customers and proactively identify new AI deployment opportunities
- Travel frequently (25-50%) to customer sites
- Champion Anthropic's mission in the field

You Might Be a Good Fit If You Have

- Security clearance not required, but a plus
- Must have prior experience working with US federal civilian, state, or local agencies
- 4+ years in a technical, customer-facing role (FDE, SWE with consulting experience, or former technical founder)
- Production experience with LLMs: advanced prompt engineering, agent development, evaluation frameworks, deployment at scale
- Strong programming skills in Python (ideally also TypeScript, Java, etc) with production shipping experience
- High agency with ability to navigate ambiguity in complex organizations
- High cooperation mindset for cross-organizational collaboration
- Passion for advancing safe, beneficial AI systems
- Strong communication skills for discovery and conveying technical concepts to diverse stakeholders

Annual Salary: $280,000—$320,000 USD
Location: Washington, DC (Hybrid, 25% in-office minimum)
